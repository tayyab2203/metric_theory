6. Bundled Product

 

6.1 Bundle Products consist of orders of 1 to 3 products. Users may not necessarily receive 3 bundled products. Regular product orders will be randomly assigned by the system. When a bundle is encountered, there is a higher chance that the User will receive a bundle products of 1 or 2 products.

 

6.2 For each product order in a bundled product, the user will receive 15-100 times the normal product commission.

 

6.3 Upon receipt of your bundled product, all funds will be returned to your account upon completion of the order for each product in the bundled product.

 

6.4 The system will randomly assign the bundled product to the User's account based on the total balance of the User's account.

 

6.5 Bundled products cannot be cancelled or skipped once they have been assigned to the user account.
